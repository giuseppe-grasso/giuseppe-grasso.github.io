Giuseppe Grasso

Postdoctoral Researcher

Instituto de Ciências Sociais (ICS-ULisboa)

University of Lisbon

 

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Lisbon, Instituto de Ciências Sociais (ICS-ULisboa). Currently, I am a Visiting Research Scholar at the University of California, Berkeley, for the 2024/2025 academic year. I obtained my Ph.D. in Economics in 2023 from the University of Luxembourg and LISER.

My research focuses on labor economics and applied micro-econometrics. My main areas of interest include skills, employment protection, inequality, and migration.
