Welcome to my website!

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Instituto de Ciências Sociais of the University of Lisbon (ICS-ULisboa). I obtained my PhD in Economics in 2023 from the University of Luxembourg and LISER.

I conduct empirical research in labor economics. My research examines the variation of skill demand across labor markets, its reaction to labor market policies, and the way it influences wage inequality. I am also interested in studying how wage inequality is affected by education, institutions, and firm policies.
